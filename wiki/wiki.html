1. Overview

Among Us is a multiplayer social deduction game. Players are aboard a spaceship, space station, or outpost, completing tasks to win while some players are secretly impostors trying to sabotage and eliminate the crew.

  • Players: 4–10 normally, but can be extended in custom/chaos modes.
  • Roles: Crewmates, Impostors, optional special roles like Scientists and Engineers.
  • Win Conditions:
    • Crewmates: Complete all tasks or eject all impostors.
    • Impostors: Kill enough crewmates to match impostor numbers, or fail critical sabotages.

2. Map System

Maps are series of interconnected rooms and hallways with tasks and vents. Common maps include:

  • The Skeld: Standard spaceship map.
  • MIRA HQ: Smaller, tighter corridors.
  • Polus: Outpost with open spaces.
  • Airship: Large multi-floor map with ladders and complex layout.

Key elements:

  • Rooms: Contain tasks or vents.
  • Hallways: Connect rooms.
  • Vents: Only impostors (or Engineers) can use to move quickly.
  • Cameras/Security: Show activity in certain rooms.

3. Roles & Abilities

Crewmates

  • Complete tasks to win.
  • Report dead bodies and call emergency meetings.
  • Cannot use vents.
  • Taskbar shows progress.

Impostors

  • Eliminate crewmates secretly.
  • Use vents, sabotage systems, and fake tasks.
  • Kill crewmates and avoid being seen.

Special Roles

  • Scientist: Can see vitals of other players.
  • Engineer: Can vent like impostors and repair faster.
  • Guardian Angel: Protects living players after death (Airship map).

4. Task System

Tasks are mini-games that crewmates complete to progress:

  • Short Tasks: Quick, easy tasks.
  • Long Tasks: More challenging, take longer.
  • Common Tasks: Shared by all crewmates.
  • Visual Tasks: Provide visual animations for others to see.

Examples of tasks:

Calibrate Distributor
Fix Wiring
Upload/Download Data
Empty Garbage
Clear Asteroids
Stabilize Steering
Chart Course

5. Sabotage System

Impostors can sabotage crewmates to create urgent problems:

  • Lights: Reduces visibility.
  • Oxygen: Crewmates must fix before timer expires.
  • Reactor: Critical, requires multiple crewmates to fix.
  • Communications: Blocks task map.
  • Doors: Locks rooms to trap players.

6. Venting System

Exclusive to impostors (and Engineers in some mods). Vents allow instant movement across the map.

  • Move between vent nodes.
  • Escape after a kill.
  • Risk: If seen, players will suspect you.

7. Meetings & Voting

Meetings occur when bodies are reported or emergency buttons pressed:

  • Players discuss suspicious behavior.
  • Vote to eject or skip.
  • Dead players can observe but not vote.
  • Strategy: Bluff, defend, and accuse to manipulate votes.

8. Advanced Mechanics

  • Visual Tasks: Can verify real task progress of crewmates.
  • Security Cameras: Watch room activity.
  • Vitals: Scientists can monitor health/status in real time.
  • Taskbar Verification: Impostors fake tasks, but the bar doesn’t move if you watch.

9. Ultimate Chaos Mode

Custom mode with insane numbers of players and impostors:

  • Players: 100+
  • Impostors: 8+
  • Rapid task completions and mass reporting.
  • Discord spam/rate limits occur with large lobbies.

10. Discord Bot Mechanics

Custom Among Us bots can automate lobbies:

  • Commands: /create, /join, /start, /vote, /fixsabotage.
  • Assign roles and tasks automatically.
  • Track events, kills, and meetings in chat.
  • Rate-limited if messages flood too quickly.
